More Pictures!

Cannoli

This is Cannoli! She is the oldest of our bunch. She is a Husky/Shepherd mix and around 6 or 7 years old. She's very energetic, and loves to play and run! What she does not love are baths and being cuddled, but we are hoping to change her mind on both of them!

Daisy

Daisy is the much calmer pup. While she likes to play as well, she does know when to stop and also really enjoys just laying around sometimes. She is our total lovebug and cannot get enough cuddles! She also loves to eat....a lot.

The Crabbies
