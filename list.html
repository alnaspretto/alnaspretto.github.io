My Favorite Books

I love to read! Reading has always been a big part of life. For as long as I could remember, I always had my nose in a book. It's one of my favorite hobbies and here are some of my favorite books!

Title Author Summary
1 Elixir Hilary Duff Growing up a 90s kid, of course I am huge fan of Hilary Duff! So it was absolutely no surprise that I was just as much of a fan of her fiction books as I am of her. In the Elixir trilogy, Clea falls in love with the mysterious Sage only to find it's in her best interest to stay away from him. The two are soulmates who come together in every lifetime, but each one ends in a horrible way for her. She's determined to make this time different, while he's trying to fight his feelings and stay away so she can stay safe.
2 The Good Girl Mary Kubica Mia goes missing for more than two months after leaving a bar with a stranger one night. Her family, friends and community are very worried as they search all over for her, unsure if she's safe or even alive. As the story goes on, it's revealed Mia arranged for herself to be kidnapped after feeling her family didn't care enough about her. The original plan was for her abudctors to reach out to her wealthy parents for a ransom they would split with her, but everything goes off the rails when they don't play along.
3 Eat, Pray, Love Elizabeth Gilbert After a divorce and a heartbreaking realization she doesn't know how to be alone, Liz Gilbert sets out on an exploration of the world and herself. Starting in Italy, she teaches herself to eat all the best foods. Then she stops in India where she spends a few months at an ashram practicing meditation. Her final stop is Bali where she just so happens to meet Javier and falls in love again.
